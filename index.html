SLITHIFY GUIDE

🎮 HOW TO PLAY

Guide the snake to eat the themed treats while avoiding walls and your own tail!

  • Desktop: Use W / A / S / D or ↑ / ↓ / ← / →. Press Space to pause/resume.
  • Mobile: Tap the D-pad arrows below the screen or swipe on the screen. Tap center ⧗ to pause/resume.

🌈 CUTE THEMES

🥞
Doraemon: Eat delicious Dora Cakes! Avoid blue brick walls.
🍪
Squid Game: Nibble Dalgona Candy and avoid pink hazard stripes.
🍬
Shinchan: Snack on Pink Chocobi Stars and dodge garden hedges.
🧀
Tom & Jerry: Grab Cheese Slices! Dodge wooden obstacles.

💡 TIPS & TRICKS

  • ✨ Mid-Game Theme Switch: Swap themes instantly using the pill buttons above the game screen!
  • ⏳ Safe Resume: Pausing and resuming gives you a 3-second safety timer before action starts.
  • 🌟 Golden Treats: Look out for glowing golden foods that give triple points, but eat them fast!
